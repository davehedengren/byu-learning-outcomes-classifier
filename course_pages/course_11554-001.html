Special Topics in Theatre Research

Theatre and Media Arts College of Fine Arts and Communications

Course Description

Special topics in focused historical, theoretical, or critical areas of performance research.

Title

Conduct Original Research

Learning Outcome

Students will conduct original research under the guidance of an expert in the subject area.

Title

Professional Development

Learning Outcome

Students will become aware of the various ethical considerations that scholarship demands.

Title

Stay Current in Field

Learning Outcome

Students will become aware of and explore newly devloping ideas in theatre and performance studies.

Title

Practical Experience

Learning Outcome

Students will gain in-depth practical experience in performance/research practices and methodologies not similarly treated elsewhere in the TMA curriculum.

Title

Develop Research Strategies

Learning Outcome

Students will experience how a professional scholar deals with various research-related concerns: archives, methodologies, current academic conversations, etc.
